Privacy Policy – Paintee

Last updated: July 5, 2025

Paintee is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

  • Camera access: Used to display your face in real-time for face painting features. We do not store any camera footage.
  • Photo library access: Used only when you save the face painting result to your device. We do not access your photo library without your permission.
  • Motion data (CoreMotion): Used to detect device movement and improve face painting stability. No motion data is stored or transmitted.
  • Face detection (Vision framework): Used to detect facial landmarks and assist with accurate painting guidance. This process runs on-device and is not stored or shared.

2. Use of Information

The information is used solely to enable the core functionality of the app:

  • Live face painting experience with camera and facial tracking
  • Saving painted results to your gallery
  • Improving the accuracy and responsiveness of the live face painting features

3. Data Storage and Security

Paintee does not store or transmit any personal data. All processing occurs locally on your device. Saved photos are stored only in your device’s photo library at your request.

4. Third-Party Services

Paintee does not use third-party analytics, ads, or tracking. No data is shared with third parties.

5. User Rights

You have the right to:

  • Control app permissions for camera, photo library, and motion access via your device settings
  • Contact us with any questions or concerns regarding your privacy

6. Policy Changes

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any updates or changes.
